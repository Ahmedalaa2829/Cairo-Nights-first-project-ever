About Cairo Nights

It’s an application which collects all the events and concerts that happens in Cairo with all it’s information so that the user have the full detail about any event he is looking for, And also the application have another section to create meet ups in categories people interested at like music,dancing,technology and learning as an example if I wanna practice another language like English or Spanish I can create a conversation club in the meet up in this app so everyone who wants to practice English or Spanish.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

Collect every event happens in Cairo in one platform and try to gather people with same interest together,Our mission is to show the people the best side of Cairo

Vision

Gathering people with same interest to complete each other and make the people and the world see the other side of Egypt
